About Carrillo's Services

Carrillo's Services is a locally owned and operated business based in Brooklyn Park, Minnesota, proudly serving the Twin Cities and surrounding areas. With years of hands-on experience in MN landscaping, junkyard removal, and property maintenance, we’ve built a reputation for reliability, affordability, and exceptional service.

Our mission is simple: to help homeowners and businesses maintain clean, beautiful, and functional outdoor spaces. Whether you need seasonal yard cleanup, debris removal, or full-scale landscaping design, our team is equipped to handle it all with professionalism and care.

We understand the unique challenges of Minnesota weather and terrain, and we tailor our services to meet the needs of each client. From snow removal in the winter to lawn care in the summer, Carrillo's Services is your year-round partner in property upkeep.

We’re proud to be part of the Minnesota community, and we treat every project—big or small—with the respect and attention it deserves. Let us help you reclaim your space and keep your property looking its best.
